THE MINIMALIST DESIGN

Minimalist design is a philosophy centered on the "less is more" principle, focusing on simplicity, functionality, and the removal of unnecessary elements to create clean, uncluttered spaces or visuals. It uses intentional, sparse, and high-quality elements—such as neutral color palettes, clean lines, and ample whitespace—to emphasize elegance, harmony, and purpose.
